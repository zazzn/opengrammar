Manual Review Checklist

Use while testing
  • Does a local issue underline quickly after typing?
  • Does the AI badge remain available when local finds nothing?
  • Does clicking the badge produce useful sentence-level fixes?
  • Do protected spans stay unchanged?
  • Does Accept apply the exact current range only?
  • Does Skip/Dismiss prevent repeated annoyance?
  • Does Polish/Formal/Casual stay inside the viewport?
  • Does native browser spellcheck conflict with OGrammar?
